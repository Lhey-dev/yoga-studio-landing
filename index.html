Classes for Every Body

Whether you crave movement, stillness, or something in between, we have a class designed to meet you where you are.

Vinyasa Flow

60 min · All levels

Restorative Yoga

75 min · Beginner friendly

Slow down and surrender. Supported poses held with props help release tension, calm the nervous system, and restore deep rest to body and mind.

  • Relaxation
  • Stress relief
  • Healing

Yin Yoga

90 min · All levels

Long-held floor poses target connective tissue and deepen flexibility. A meditative practice that cultivates patience, presence, and inner quiet.

  • Deep stretch
  • Meditation
  • Mobility

Meet Your Guide

Maya Chen

Lead Instructor & Studio Founder

Maya discovered yoga fifteen years ago while recovering from a running injury. What began as physical therapy became a lifelong path of self-discovery. She holds certifications in Vinyasa, Yin, and Restorative yoga, and has trained with teachers in India, Bali, and California.

At Serenity Flow, Maya creates a judgment-free space where students feel safe to explore, stumble, and grow. Her teaching blends precise alignment with compassionate encouragement — always honoring the wisdom of your own body.

"Yoga isn't about touching your toes. It's about what you learn on the way down."
